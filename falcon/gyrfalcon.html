    Diet

    Gyrfalcons, like other falcons, eat mainly other birds. They prefer to hunt ground birds such as ptarmigan and grouse but also hunt seabirds and waterfowl, as well as land mammals such as ground squirrels, lemmings, voles, hares, rabbits, and marmots. Gyrfalcons often hunt using a fast, low flight to chase their prey.

    Fun Fact

    The gyrfalcon is the world's fastest bird in level flight.These powerful birds may initially be underestimated, but their strength becomes evident after a few seconds in the air.
