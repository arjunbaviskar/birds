Barbary Falcon

  • Length : 33-39 cm
  • Weight : 600 g - 1.3 kg
  • Wingspan : 76-98 cm
  • Scientific name: Falco pelegrinoides
  • Carnivorous

    Habitat

    The range of Barbary Falcon extends from western Morocco, eastwards across the deserts of northern Africa, through Israel, southwards into Saudi Arabia and north-eastwards to the deserts of Central Asia.

    Diet

    Woodpeckers, Passeriformes, and Bee-eater were the most frequent prey items in the diet of Barbary Falcons.

    Fun Fact

    They have excellent binocular vision, eight times better than that of a human. They can see prey from more than 3 km away. They have a third eyelid, which spreads tears and clears debris away to protect their eyes without obstructing their vision during a stoop.
